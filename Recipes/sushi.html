Sushi

I really don't remember how to do this one

Buying sushi at the shops is always too expensive for what you get. So you think you'll be a smarty pants and make it yourself. Well in just a few short hours, you'll have a newfound appreciation for supermarket sushi.

If this is cultural appropriation I am so sorry.

Ingredients

  • Sushi rice, yes there's a special type
  • Rice vinegar
  • Sugar
  • Sauce of some description e.g. teriyaki, guacamole or even ketchup if you really want people (food snobs) to lynch you.
  • Cucumber
  • Carrot
  • Seaweed sheets

Methodology

  1. Cook the rice the special sushi way which means letting it cool and forget how to do it properly halfway through writing this and copy and paste a recipe from online.
  2. Thoroughly rinse rice until water runs clear
  3. Cook rice according to steamer instructions
  4. Let rice thoroughly cool and transfer to nonmetallic bowl
  5. Mix vinegar, sugar, and salt until dissolved and pour into rice
  6. Gently mix rice to incorporate seasoning
  7. Prepare veggies and cut into strips
  8. Be sure and deseed cucumber
  9. With rough side facing up, apply rice to nori sheet covering all edges.

  11. Layer veggies on botton of nori and with a mat, or a towel carefully roll nori keeping all veggies tucked inside roll.
  12. Slice with a wet knife.
  13. Keep hands and knife rinsed from sticky rice at all times.
  14. Season with tastes sesame seeds if desired.
  15. Use Soy Sauce and Thai Sweet Chile Sauce for dipping.
