Cashew Cream

...and its many uses

You might have a problem with creaming cashews. "They don't have nipples, how can they be creamed?" smirks the unassuming victim of a brutal rebuttal who has neglected to recall the existence of peanut butter or coconut milk.

If this sounds like you, don't worry, there's hope you'll yet know love. Just cream those cashews (literally just blend them).

Ingredients

  • Cashews
  • Nutritional Yeast
  • Juice of lemon
  • A blender
  • Does water count?

Methodology

  1. Soak cashews for a period of time, unspecified here (try asking your cashews when they might be finished soaking)
  2. Rip cashews away from their precious soaking water (drain them)
  3. Squeeze a single cashew between your fingers
  4. Bask in the supple yet callous display of your power. You are the master of your cashews.
  5. Blend all ingredients together and eat.
  6. Oh don't forget to season and also add some mustard.

Notes

Slop it in your lasagne as a white sauce, slap it in your pasta to make a creamy bechamel sauce, or slonk it up with slightly less water and baby you got a dip going.
