Cashew Cream

...and its many uses

You might have a problem with creaming cashews. "They don't have nipples, how can they be creamed?" smirks the unassuming victim of a brutal rebuttal who has neglected to consider peanut butter when concocting their latest recyled smirk.

If this sounds like you, don't worry, there's hope you'll yet know love. Just cream these cashews.

Ingredients

  • Cashews
  • Nutritional Yeast
  • Juice of lemon
  • A blender
  • Does water count?

Methodology

  1. Soak cashews for a period of time, unspecified here (try asking your cashews when they might be finished soaking)
  2. Rip cashews away from their precious soaking water (drain them)
  3. Squeeze a single cashew between your fingers
  4. Bask in the supple yet callous display of your power. You are the master of your cashews.
  5. Blend all ingredients together and eat.
  6. Oh don't forget to season and also add some mustard.
